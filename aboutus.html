About Us
Modernasing recruitment through subscription

RecruitCode is a Hyderabad-based start-up born to disrupt the permanent recruitment industry in India through collaborative and flexible approach. Recruitcode helps companies of all sizes grow their business and teams through high-impact talent acquisition programs.

The subscription economy has changed consumer expectations. Innovators like Amazon Prime, Uber, Salesforce and Box have transformed transportation, software, cloud storage, product delivery etc. Now business owners want similar convenience in every area of their business.

So, we asked ourselves - what if businesses could subscribe to staff and get started straight away, in a way that's convenient and compliant? That’s how the concept of Recruitcode was born.

We are the first subscription based, full service talent acquisition solutions provider in India. We provide flexible, scalable managed recruitment services to help you hire the right people, and build talent pipelines for today and the future. We service clients across India and manage all levels of recruitment.

Why RecruitCode

Passion for results - We are passionate about providing the best in strategy and execution. We help companies think more strategically about how they attract the right people to grow their business. We have a genuine passion for driving results, thinking out-of-the-box and building strategic relationships. Our programs measure and analyze everything to help you gain visibility into your hiring metrics as well as optimize your investments.
